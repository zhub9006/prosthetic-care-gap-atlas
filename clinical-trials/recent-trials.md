### 2. Prosthetic Socket Fit & Residual Limb Volume Research (Multi-Site)
- **Status**: Various (active research program)
- **Principal Investigators**: Sanders JE, Allyn KJ, Hafner BJ
- **Institution**: Principal investigator group (US-based, multiple sites)
- **Focus Areas**:
  - Post-doffing residual limb fluid volume change in trans-tibial amputees
  - Elevated vacuum effects on in-socket residual limb fluid volume
  - Novel bladder-liner for facilitating fluid volume recovery without doffing
  - Instrumented socket inserts for sensing limb-socket interface
  - Activity intensity, time, and intermittent doffing effects on daily limb fluid volume
- **Key References** (*Prosthet Orthot Int*):
  - Sanders JE et al. (2009): Post-doffing residual limb fluid volume change
  - Sanders JE et al. (2011): Elevated vacuum effects on residual limb fluid volume
  - Sanders JE et al. (2015): Temporary socket removal effects
  - Sanders JE et al. (2016): Novel bladder-liner evaluation
  - Sanders JE et al. (2018): Residual limb fluid volume change and volume accommodation
  - Youngblood RT et al. (2019): Activity intensity effects on limb fluid volume
  - Swanson EC et al. (2018): Instrumented socket inserts
  - Gailey R et al. (2019): Comprehensive lower limb amputee socket survey
