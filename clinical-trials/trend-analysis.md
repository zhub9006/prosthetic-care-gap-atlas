# Clinical Trials Trend Analysis: Prosthetic & Orthotic Research

## Data Source

ClinicalTrials.gov API — search term: "prosthetic" and "prosthetic limb"
Analysis date: 2026-06-29

--- 

## Trials by Status (Global)

Total prosthetic-related trials identified: **2,169**

| Status | Count | Percentage | Interpretation |
|--------|-------|------------|----------------|
| RECRUITING | 381 | 17.6% | Active enrollment — highest priority for partnership |
| COMPLETED | 926 | 42.7% | Results may be available; potential for implementation |
| NOT_YET_RECRUITING | 143 | 6.6% | Future studies — monitor for opening |
| UNKNOWN | 430 | 19.9% | Status unclear — may be abandoned or pending |
| ACTIVE_NOT_RECRUITING | 120 | 5.5% | Ongoing but not enrolling new participants |
| ENROLLING_BY_INVITATION | 41 | 1.9% | Targeted recruitment |
| WITHDRAWN | 45 | 2.1% | Studies stopped early |
| TERMINATED | 76 | 3.5% | Prematurely ended |
| SUSPENDED | 5 | 0.2% | Temporarily paused |

### Key Insight
Only **17.6%** of prosthetic trials are actively recruiting. The large number of **UNKNOWN** status trials (19.9%) and **COMPLETED** trials (42.7%) suggests a significant gap between research completion and result dissemination/implementation.

## Gap Analysis: Research vs. Access

| Metric | Finding |
|--------|---------|
| Global prosthetic trials | 2,169+ |
| US-centric trials | 48.1% of total |
| Actively recruiting trials | 17.6% |
| Prosthetic specialists in target regions | 0 |
| Rehabilitation clinics in target regions | 2-3 per region |
| Pharmacies (primary healthcare touchpoint) | 10-20 per region |

**Critical Disconnect**: The US produces nearly half of all global prosthetic research, yet the regions with the greatest need (rural, Appalachian, Delta) have **no prosthetic/orthotic specialist infrastructure** to translate that research into patient care.
